A'ja Riyadh Wilson

Professional Basketballer

Description

A'ja is an American professional Basketball player for Las Vegas Aces of the Women National Basketball Association (WNBA)

Wilson played for the South Carolina Gamecocks in college, and helped lead the Gamecocks to their first NCAA Women's Basketball Championship in 2017, and won the NCAA basketball tournament Most Outstanding Player award. In 2018, she won a record 3rd straight SEC Player of the Year award, leading South Carolina to a record 4th straight SEC Tournament Championship, becoming the all-time leading scorer in South Carolina women's basketball history, and was a consensus first-team All-American for the 3rd consecutive season.

Awards

  • WNBA Most Valuable Player (2020, 2022)
  • WNBA Defensive Player of the Year (2022)
